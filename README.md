# Thailand Oil Price Analysis ( My first tried for analysis)

## Overview

This project analyzes Brent crude oil price trends from June 2025 to March 2026 and discusses the potential economic impact on Thailand, including GDP growth and inflation.

## Objectives

* Analyze Brent crude oil price trend
* Identify significant changes
* Estimate potential impact on Thailand's economy
* Visualize data for clear interpretation

## Data Source

* Brent crude oil price data (monthly)
* Public economic references on oil price impact

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Basic statistical analysis
* Data visualization (line chart)

## Key Findings

* Oil prices were relatively stable from June 2025 to early 2026
* Prices increased significantly in March 2026
* A $10 increase in oil prices may reduce Thailand’s GDP growth by approximately 0.2–0.4 percentage points
* Higher oil prices may contribute to inflation pressure

## Files in Repository

* `oil_analysis.xlsx.xls` — raw data
* `Brent_Oil_Thailand_Analysis_2026.pdf` — visualization
